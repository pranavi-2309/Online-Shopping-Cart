Return & Refund Policy

Return Window

You have 7 days from the date of delivery to initiate a return for most products.

Eligible Products

  • Products must be unused and in original packaging
  • All tags and labels must be intact
  • No damage or alterations to the product
  • Undergarments, cosmetics, and perishables are non-returnable

Return Process

  1. Go to My Orders and select the product
  2. Click on Return and choose reason
  3. Free pickup will be scheduled within 2-3 business days
  4. Quality check will be done after product is received

Refund Timeline

  • Refund initiated within 2-3 days after quality check
  • Amount credited within 5-7 business days
  • Original payment method will be credited

Exchange Policy

For size/color exchanges, we offer free exchange within 7 days. Exchange product will be shipped after receiving the original item.
